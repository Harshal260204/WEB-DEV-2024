SCCT NSS CAMP, Mulgaon Badlapur

In Jan 2022, 50 Volunteers From NSS unit Of sanpada college of commerce and technology visited mulgaon badlapur for a 7 days NSS camp. In those 7 days they executed various activites including street play,trek to the khandoba temple mulgaon ,serving people at the khandoba festival, Cleaning the surrounding of the mulgaon camp, fought with electricity crisis and much more

  1. Cleanliness Drive In Mulgaon

    NSS Volunteers conducted a Cleanliness drive to make sure that the surrounding is clean.

  2. Served More Than 1600 People In Khandoba festival

    NSS Volunteers Served More Than 1600 People In The Khandoba Festival.
