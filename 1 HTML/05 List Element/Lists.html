Angelas Recipe For The Best Cinnamon Rolls

Ingredients

For The Dough :

  • 3/4 cup warm milk
  • 2 1/4 teaspoons yeast
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 egg plus 1 egg yolk
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 3 cups bread flour

For The Filling :

  • 2/3 cup dark brown sugar
  • 1 1/2 tablespoon ground Cinnamon
  • 1/4 cup butter

Instructions :

  1. mix the milk with sugar,yeast,eggs
  2. melt the butter and add to the mixture
  3. Add in the flour and mix until combined into a dough
  4. knead the dough for 10 minutes
  5. transfer the dough into a large bowl and cover with plastix wrap. Leave it somwhere to rise for 2 hours
